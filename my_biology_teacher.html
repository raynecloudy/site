my biology teacher

over the winter break of 2024/2025, my biology teacher passed away from a heart attack. i wrote this poem for him.

written: 5th of january, 2025

my biology teacher recently passed away
i really don't know what to say
i never really knew him well but
i had his class, hey

i wish i had been more attentive in class
but now it's too late
myself, i kind of hate

if my phone had never been out
i probably would've had a pout
because my phone is apparantly more important
than the man who lovingly shouts

my biology teacher was loud
always encouraged us to talk
for that, i always walked

now that he's gone, i feel much guilt
for the memories i never built
so starting today, i'm going to
put away the hilt

i'm gonna put the phone down
and make some new friends
that way, his legacy never ends

rest in peace.
