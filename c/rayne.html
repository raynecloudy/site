rayne is my persona. he's 15, uses he/they pronouns, and is a femboy.

lore

rayne is the encompassing entity of hayden and hayley's personalities. he depicts traits of both personalities, both psychologically (f.e. mellow, like hayden, but also happy-go-lucky at times, like hayley) and physically (f.e. hayden's hairstyle, hayley's hairpin).

note that he does NOT have multiple personality disorder- he is the result of hayden and hayley being merged into one person and sense of self.

before hayley, it was just hayden. hayden shares all memories with rayne because they are both part of the same being. therefore, rayne remembers hayden's identity crisis.

appearance

physical

he's biologically male- he doesn't have feminine features! please keep this in mind!!!! however, he does have soft shoulders.

his hair is naturally a light coffee brown color. he doesn't have hair anywhere but on the top of his head!

his eyes are light pink.

cosmetic

rayne has dyed hair. it doesn't really matter what color dye you draw. go wild :3

i try to put rayne in a different outfit every time i draw him. don't worry about a set wardrobe when drawing him, just don't do anything discriminatory or too edgy iykwim. don't be afraid to put a cute design on the shirt though!!

he has a silly lil star accessory in his hair :3 it can be purple or pink you decide
